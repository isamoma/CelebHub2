Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CelebHub (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website or submit your celebrity profile. By accessing or using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Social media links
  • Biography and category of talent
  • Payment details (via MPESA STK Push only)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify celebrity profile submissions.
  • To display your public profile on CelebHub once approved.
  • To contact you regarding your submission.
  • To process MPESA payments securely for featured listing.

3. MPESA Payment Data

All payments are processed through Safaricom MPESA STK Push. We do not store your MPESA PIN. All payment processing follows Safaricom’s security requirements.

4. Data Protection & Security

We follow Kenya’s Data Protection Act 2019. All your information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.

5. Cookies

We use analytics and session cookies to improve your browsing experience.

  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
